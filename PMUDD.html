User Data Deletion Policy

1. Introduction

We are committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of our users. This User Data Deletion Policy outlines the procedures and rights related to the deletion of personal data collected and processed by [Your Company Name].

2. Scope

This policy applies to all users of our services and covers all personal data collected, stored, and processed by [Your Company Name] through our website, mobile applications, and other digital platforms.

3. User Rights

Users have the right to request the deletion of their personal data at any time. This includes:

  • Account information
  • Profile data
  • Usage history
  • Communication records
  • Any other personally identifiable information (PII)

4. How to Request Data Deletion

Users can request data deletion by:

  • Sending an email to: [privacy@yourcompany.com]
  • Using the in-app “Delete My Data” feature (if available)
  • Submitting a request through our [Privacy Center / Contact Form]

Requests must include:

  • Full name
  • Registered email address
  • Description of the data to be deleted (if partial deletion is requested)

5. Verification Process

To protect user privacy, we may require identity verification before processing a deletion request. This may include:

  • Email confirmation
  • Two-factor authentication
  • Additional identity verification steps if necessary

6. Processing Timeline

We aim to process all verified data deletion requests within 30 days. In some cases, this period may be extended due to complexity or legal obligations, in which case the user will be notified.

7. Exceptions

We may retain certain data if required by:

  • Legal or regulatory obligations
  • Fraud prevention and security purposes
  • Financial recordkeeping (e.g., transaction history)

8. Data Deletion Methods

Data will be deleted using secure methods, including:

  • Permanent removal from active databases
  • Anonymization or pseudonymization where appropriate
  • Secure deletion from backups within a reasonable timeframe
